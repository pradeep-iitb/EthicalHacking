# Introduction to Cybersecurity Essentials

"Introduction to Cybersecurity Essentials" provides foundational knowledge and skills necessary for various roles in cybersecurity.

## Course Overview

* Focuses on **essential cybersecurity concepts**, tools, and best practices.
* Addresses the evolving threat landscape, including:
    * Data theft
    * Phishing
    * Malware

## Key Learning Objectives

* **Identify common security risks** and methods to defend against cyberattacks.
* Understand the importance of **information privacy** and effective password management.

## Practical Skills Development

* Engage in **hands-on labs** and interactive activities to reinforce learning.
* Prepare for **CompTIA IT Fundamentals (ITF+) Certification** as part of the course series.
